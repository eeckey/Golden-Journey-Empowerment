Breastfeeding Facts

Black babies are dying at twice the rate (in some place, nearly triple) the rate of white babies.

The high infant mortality rate among black infants is mostly due to being disproportionately born too small, too sick or too soon.

According to the CDC, increased breastfeeding among black women could decrease infant mortality rates by as much as 50%.
